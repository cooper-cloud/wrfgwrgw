About

High-signal junior mining media.

Ore Media is an independent platform focused on long-form interviews and analysis across junior mining and resource markets.

What Ore Media is

Ore Media provides structured, fundamentals-driven interviews that emphasize assets, execution, capital structure, and strategy. The goal is to produce content that is useful for serious market participants — not promotional noise.

Editorial position

Ore Media is editorially independent and unaffiliated with brokers, promoters, or investor relations firms. Ore Media is not a marketing agency. Interviews are editorial in nature.

Coverage focus

Publicly listed junior miners (ASX / TSXV / AIM), including exploration and development-stage stories, optionality vehicles, and companies navigating the capital markets.

Company interview requests

If you can speak substantively about the asset and strategy, apply.
